I know you have probably read this somewhere else before but let’s bring it back.
Can we talk about how these ai models are making us lose our selves?
 Like personally ever since I started using chat I can’t even push my codes on git without using the git codes provided by chat mind you I know the commands but I always feel unsure when I am writing them than copying and pasting them directly from the chat. 

At this point it’s not even about certainty it’s about confidence. These ai models make us so dependent that we feel unsure of ourselves every time we are not using them.

And honestly if you can related to this, I feel you. I know how it feel like to always steal a glance on the codes provided by chatgpt to make sure that yours are good, how to prompt something to respond to someone because you feel like ai is more compassionate with them than you, giving ai your writings to make them sound better to only be changed into something completely different from what you were thinking and keeping it like that because you trust what it gives you more than what comes out of you.

Ai is slowly and forcefully stealing the trust we had for ourselves, making us feel unstable and incomplete without it. And if this is not scary to you I don’t know the clay you are molded in.
