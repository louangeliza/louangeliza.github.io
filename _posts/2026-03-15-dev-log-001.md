It was working yesterday.

started the backend, everything looked fine at first. no errors, nothing crashing… which honestly should’ve been my first red flag 😭  
whenever things work too smoothly, something is hiding.

one endpoint was acting weird. not fully broken, just giving the wrong data. and those are the worst bugs because nothing looks wrong. no errors, no warnings, just vibes being off.

spent way too long going through the logic, checking queries, even questioning if i broke something else earlier. turns out it was just one condition messing everything up. one line.

fixed it. tested again. it worked.

felt like a genius for about 2 minutes.

then something else broke.

at this point i don’t even react anymore 😭 it’s like every fix just reveals the next problem waiting in line. like the system is saying “okay cool, you passed level 1… here’s level 2.”

had to restart stuff, re-run things, double-check configs. you know that phase where you’re not even sure what you’re looking for anymore, you’re just trying things hoping something clicks.

eventually got it stable-ish. not perfect, but good enough to move on.

and i’m starting to realize that’s basically what building is. nothing is ever really “done.” it just reaches a point where it works enough and you keep going.

but yeah, small wins today. fixed a bug, understood the flow a bit better, cleaned up some parts that were bothering me.

still feels like there’s a lot i don’t know yet… but i guess that’s part of it.

slow progress > no progress.

tomorrow we go again.
